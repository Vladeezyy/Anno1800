This mod offers additional powerplants:

Gaspowerplant for Enbesa, New World, Arctic
Fishoilpowerplant for New world
Woodpowerplant for Old World, New World
Nuclear powerplant for Old World, New World
Oilpowerplant Enbesa, Arctic
Coalpowerplant for Old World, New World, Arctic

The gaspowerplant of the Old World is already available with engineers, but still requires the DLC The Passage.

For Uranium open pit you have to use clay pits.

Though it's shown in the docklands, it's not sellable because of the negative price.
Nuclear waste can only be sold to the traders, but you have to pay for the disposal: 50.000/t.
